About

Due Dissidence began as a blog and podcast project in 2019 - we are now a multimedia one-stop shop for political commentary, interviews, and satire.

Our videos and livestreams can be viewed on YouTube and Rumble. Written articles appear on Substack. Audio versions of our streams are available on all major podcast players.

We are a completely independent outlet, and so support on Patreon and/or Substack is much appreciated.

To get in touch with us for questions, comments, feedback, or press inquiries, send us an email at duedissidence@gmail.com.
